WELCOME TO MEMBERSHPE UF

Welcome to MemberSHPE UF! Register and enjoy all the benefits available to you. You'll no longer have to make a line to sign in nor type all of your information at every SHPE UF event. Register once, login and type the event code to redeem your points. You'll be able to review the Points Rewards System and keep track of your points. Additionally, you'll also be able to find and get in contact with SHPE UF alumni willing to help and guide you. Gone are the days of sending your résumé via email for BBQ with Industry or any other professional, career-building event; upload your résumé to the Résumé Repository once and we'll take care of the rest.

Rewards Program

Get familiarized with the SHPE UF Incentives Program and Points Rewards system. You'll no longer need to wait in line to sign in. Login from your laptop, phone, anything with an internet connection, and enter the event code to redeem your points. View your events and points and see where you stand in comparison to everyone else in SHPE UF.

Alumni Directory

Got an internship? Got question about your major? Just moved and want to know what's there to do in town? Get in contact with one of the many SHPE UF alumni willing to help you. You'll be able to search the Alumni Directory according to major, company, and location. Connect with alumni and learn from their experiences.

Résumé Repository

(COMING SOON)

We have all forgotten to submit something by a deadline, don't let your résumé be one of those things. Submit your résumé to the Résumé Repository once and it's done. Your résumé will be sent to recruiters for events like BBQ with Industry and every career development event.
